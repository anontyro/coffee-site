About Us

"Each can of coffee represents an imaginary voyage – Head for the Hills”

Greenhills Coffee is a local brand that knows Myanmar and knows coffee like no one else. A great cup of coffee is an art form with the capability of stirring wonderful emotions, whilst providing comfort for the mind and soul. We know that YOU – Myanmar deserve a finely crafted can of coffee which can revitalise your exhausted soul and soothe your daily worries.

We are dedicated to bringing Myanmar the premium coffee experience that you deserve at an affordable price, conveniently to your local shops. To cater for the different tastes we currently offer four different coffee products which vary in amounts of coffee, milk and extra flavours including the ever popular vanilla and chocolate.

Our Products

“Black Coffee” is the coffee beverage without cream, milk or sugar, which is the perfect taste for the ultimate coffee lover. A can of Green Hills Black Coffee will brighten your mornings and freshen up your days.
#fullflavour

“Iced coffee”, the original milk coffee beverage, is the perfect boost to your day. Energize your day with a can of delicious Green Hills Iced Coffee.
#perfectboost

Mocha” also known as “Mocaccino”, is a chocolate flavored coffee. The taste of the perfect combination of espresso, milk, and cocoa powder/ chocolate syrup, will complete your day with happiness. A can of Greenhills Mocha will let you taste the happiness.
#Tastethehappiness

As “Latte” can be literally translated into “coffee milk”, Vanilla Latte is the rich espresso blending well with steamed milk and topped off perfectly with vanilla syrup. A can of Greenhills Vanilla Latte will add the sweetness to your life
#Sweetnesstoyourlife
